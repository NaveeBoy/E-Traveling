One World Mirissa

Set in Mirissa, a few steps from Mirissa Beach, One World Mirissa offers accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, an outdoor swimming pool and a shared lounge. Featuring family rooms, this property also provides guests with a sun terrace. The accommodation features room service, a tour desk and currency exchange for guests. At the hotel, all rooms come with a desk, a flat-screen TV, a private bathroom, bed linen and towels. Rooms are fitted with air conditioning, and certain units at One World Mirissa have a patio. A continental breakfast is available every morning at the accommodation. Thalaramba Beach is 1.1 km from One World Mirissa, while Weligambay Beach is 2 km away. The nearest airport is Koggala, 21 km from the hotel, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service. This is our guests' favourite part of Mirissa, according to independent reviews.
