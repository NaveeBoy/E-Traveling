Pandanus Beach Resort & Spa

Located on the beachfront in Induruwa, Pandanus Beach Resort & Spa features an outdoor pool, a fitness centre and a barber/beauty shop. Its rooms offer sea views and complimentary Wi-Fi access. Fitted with a balcony, rooms feature a flat-screen TV, an electric kettle and a minibar. The en suite bathroom offers shower facilities and a hairdryer. Guests can dine at the resort’s restaurant, which serve à la carte delights and buffet spreads. Room service is also provided. Staff at the 24-hour reception can assist with laundry, fax/photocopying, ironing and currency exchange services. Free private parking spaces are available. The resort is 89 km from Bandaranaike Airport. Couples particularly like the location — they rated it 8.8 for a two-person trip.
