J7 Villaj Resort

J7 Villaj Resort has a restaurant, outdoor swimming pool, a bar and garden in Trincomalee. Boasting family rooms, this property also provides guests with a terrace. The accommodation features a 24-hour front desk, room service and currency exchange for guests. At the hotel the rooms include air conditioning, a desk, a flat-screen TV, a private bathroom, bed linen, towels and a balcony with pool view. J7 Villaj Resort provides some rooms with garden views, and each room has a kettle. At the accommodation, the rooms have a seating area. Guests at J7 Villaj Resort can enjoy a buffet or a à la carte breakfast. The area is popular for cycling, and car hire is available at the hotel. Uppuveli beach is 300 m from J7 Villaj Resort, while Kanniya Hot Springs is 4.3 km from the property. The nearest airport is China Bay, 13 km from the accommodation, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service. Couples particularly like the location — they rated it 9.1 for a two-person trip.
